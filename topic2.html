History & Evolution of HTTP

Tim Berners-Lee and the Beginning of HTTP

HTTP was created in 1989 by Tim Berners-Lee, originally to share documents easily among researchers.

Versions of HTTP:

  • HTTP/0.9: Simple text documents.
  • HTTP/1.0: Added ability to handle more complex requests.
  • HTTP/1.1: Improved performance and caching.
  • HTTP/2: Multiple requests simultaneously for faster loading.
  • HTTP/3: Uses QUIC protocol (developed by Google) for even faster, secure connections.
